Money

▶ Article 265:
This article deals with the principles for taxation. It states that no tax shall be levied or collected except by authority of law.
▶ Article 266:
This article deals with the Consolidated Fund of India and the public accounts of India and of the states.
▶ Article 267:
This article deals with Contingency Fund of India.
▶ Article 268:
This article deals with the Duties levied by the Union but collected and appropriated by the States.
▶ Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934:
Defines the powers and functions of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), India's central bank, including its role in formulating monetary policy, regulating the financial system, and issuing currency.
▶ Coinage Act, 1906:
Governs the minting, issue, and regulation of coins in India.
▶ Fiscal Responsibility and Budget Management (FRBM) Act, 2003:
Aims to ensure fiscal discipline and transparency in government finances, contributing to overall macroeconomic stability.
▶ Section 403: Dishonest misappropriation of property:
This section deals with misappropriation of money or movable property by a person who is entrusted with it, or who has control over it, with the intention of dishonestly using it for their own benefit..
▶ Section 404: Dishonest misappropriation of property possessed by deceased person at the time of his death
This section deals with the misappropriation of money or property that belonged to a deceased person at the time of their death.
▶ Section 405: Criminal breach of trust:
This section deals with situations where someone, who is entrusted with property or money, dishonestly misappropriates it or converts it for their own use in violation of the trust placed in them.
▶ Section 406: Punishment for criminal breach of trust
This section prescribes punishment for the offense of criminal breach of trust, which may include imprisonment and/or a fine.
▶ Section 420: Cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property:
As mentioned earlier, this section addresses cheating and dishonestly inducing the delivery of property, including money, through fraudulent means.
