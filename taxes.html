Taxes

▶ Article 265:
Ensures that no tax shall be levied or collected except by the authority of law.
▶ Article 246:
Deals with the distribution of legislative powers between the Union and the States with respect to taxation.
▶ Article 280:
Provides for the establishment of a Finance Commission to recommend the distribution of taxes between the Union and the States.
▶ Income Tax Act, 1961:
Primary legislation governing income tax in India.
▶ Goods and Services Tax (GST) Acts:
Comprehensive acts governing the levy and collection of GST.
▶ Customs Act, 1962:
Deals with the levy and collection of customs duties on imports and exports.
▶ Central Excise Act, 1944:
Previously governed the levy and collection of excise duty on goods manufactured in India.
▶ Finance Act:
Passed annually as part of the Union Budget, incorporating provisions related to taxation.
▶ Section 279:
Deals with reckless or dangerous driving or riding on public roads.
▶ Section 304A:
Deals with causing death by negligence.
▶ Section 338:
Deals with causing grievous hurt by an act endangering life or personal safety of others.
▶ Section 427:
Deals with mischief causing damage to property.
