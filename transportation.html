Transportation

▶ Article 301:
Ensures freedom of trade, commerce, and intercourse throughout the territory of India, which impacts the regulation of transportation of goods and passengers across state boundaries.
▶ Article 302:
Grants the power to Parliament to impose restrictions on trade, commerce, and intercourse between states, which can affect interstate transportation regulations.
▶ Article 303:
Imposes restrictions on the legislative powers of the Union and the States concerning trade and commerce, which may impact transportation-related legislation.
▶ Motor Vehicles Act, 1988:
Regulates the registration, licensing, and operation of motor vehicles, as well as the conduct of drivers and rules of the road.
▶ Central Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989:
Provides detailed regulations concerning various aspects of motor vehicles, including construction, equipment, and standards for safety and emissions.
▶ Airports Authority of India Act, 1994:
Governs the management and operation of airports in India, including the regulation of air traffic and safety standards.
▶ Aircraft Act, 1934:
Regulates the registration, operation, and airworthiness of aircraft, as well as the licensing of pilots and aircrew.
▶ Inland Waterways Authority of India Act, 1985:
Provides for the regulation and development of inland waterways for navigation, including the construction and maintenance of waterways infrastructure.
▶ Merchant Shipping Act, 1958:
Regulates merchant shipping in Indian waters, including registration of ships, safety standards, and employment conditions of seafarers.
▶ Carriage by Road Act, 2007:
Governs the rights and liabilities of carriers and consignors in relation to the carriage of goods by road.
▶ Road Transport Corporations Act, 1950:
Provides for the establishment and regulation of road transport corporations for the operation of road transport services.
▶ Coast Guard Act, 1978:
Establishes the Indian Coast Guard and provides for its powers, duties, and functions related to maritime safety, security, and environmental protection.
▶ Section 279: Rash driving or riding on a public way -
This section deals with reckless or dangerous driving or riding on public roads which may endanger the safety of others.
▶ Section 304A: Causing death by negligence -
This section deals with causing death of any person by a negligent act not amounting to culpable homicide.
▶ Section 338: Causing grievous hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others -
This section deals with causing grievous hurt to any person by a negligent act endangering human life or personal safety of others.
▶ Section 427: Mischief causing damage to the amount of fifty rupees -
This section deals with causing damage to property, including vehicles.
